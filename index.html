{{ "artificial intelligence for social good" | upcase }}

CAIS++ (pronounced "case plus plus") is a USC Viterbi-affiliated undergraduate student organization that serves as the student branch of the USC Center for Artificial Intelligence in Society (CAIS). In brief, our students work to promote the development of AI applications for social good.

  • For Clients/Professors
  • For Sponsors

{{"using artificial intelligence to benefit society at large" | upcase }}

{{"promoting research that matters" | upcase}}

We believe that artificial Intelligence can and should be used to help solve society's grand challenges, especially where traditional techniques have come short.

{{"supporting productive discourse" | upcase}}

Often left out of the discussion surrounding AI is the idea that AI can be used as a force for good. We think that this positive potential deserves a place in the AI discussion.

{{"making ai accessible" | upcase}}

We feel that it is essential to educate people about the principles behind AI now, so that we can make educated technological decisions in the future.

{{"creating a community" | upcase}}

Our researchers and developers form life-long relationships that enable them to collaborate well beyond their undergraduate years.

{{"what we do" | upcase }}

{{"educate" | upcase}}

Each semester, a new cohort of members are walked through an introductory AI curriculum, complete with online lessons and in-person coding workshops.

{{"develop" | upcase}}

Members who are familiar with AI basics or have gone through our curriculum are placed into teams to apply their skills to help solve real world problems.

{{"reach out" | upcase}}

In addition to member activities, CAIS++ hosts a variety of public events designed to demystify AI and to empower and inspire students to use AI for social good.

{{"inform" | upcase}}

By inviting speakers from both academia and industry, we hope to shed light on ways in which AI can be (and already has been) used to benefit society.

{{"frequently asked questions" | upcase }}

I don't know any AI. Am I eligible to apply?

Our mission is to make artificial intelligence accessible to students from all backgrounds, wherever they might be with AI now. Though prior AI experience is not necessary, we look for members are able to demonstrate passion for research, technology, social good, and critical thinking ability.

How can I stand out from other applicants?

To demonstrate the potential of AI for social good, many of our projects demand rigorous involvement, self-motivation, and technical experience. Some activities that may demonstrate these skills are prior project experience, AI implementation, and reading research literature.

What can I expect to gain out of joining CAIS++?

We are a community of researchers, engineers, and scientists passionate about learning state of the art artificial intelligence, building machine learning projects for social good, discussing classic and novel research papers, networking with industry professionals and professors, and searching for solutions to challenging problems. If this sounds like you, we look forward to you joining us and working together towards creative and exciting technologies.

Can master students apply to CAIS++?

Unfortunately, we only accept undergraduate USC students.

When is the recruitment cycle?

CAIS++ opens up recruitment during August of every year. Unfortunately, we do not take new students in the Spring.
